About nextNtern

NextNtern is a platform that connects students and recent graduates with meaningful internship opportunities across various industries. Designed to bridge the gap between academic learning and practical experience, NextNtern helps young professionals build their careers by offering hands-on work exposure, mentorship, and networking opportunities. The platform is user-friendly, allowing candidates to create profiles, upload resumes, and apply to internships that match their skills and interests. It also supports employers by providing a curated pool of enthusiastic and qualified candidates. With a focus on fostering talent and promoting career growth, NextNtern partners with startups, established companies, and educational institutions to ensure a diverse range of opportunities. Its mission is to empower the next generation of professionals by giving them access to real-world experiences that complement their academic journey. Through NextNtern, students gain valuable insights, enhance their resumes, and take a confident step toward their professional aspirations.
